About Us

Sant Tapasvi Narayan Das Shikshan Sansthan is dedicated to providing quality education and fostering holistic development in students. Our institution is committed to academic excellence, character building, and community service.

Established with the vision to empower the youth of Sitamarhi, we offer a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs. Our experienced faculty and state-of-the-art facilities ensure that students receive the best education possible.

We believe in nurturing not just the intellect but also the values of compassion, integrity, and social responsibility. Our students are encouraged to participate in various extracurricular activities that enhance their skills and prepare them for future challenges.

Our College

Sant Tapasvi Narayan Das Shikshan Sansthan

Our college is a hub of academic and cultural activities, providing a vibrant environment for students to thrive. With a focus on innovation and research, we aim to equip our students with the skills needed to excel in their chosen fields.

We offer a wide range of programs across various disciplines, ensuring that students have access to quality education that meets the demands of the modern world. Our faculty members are dedicated to mentoring and guiding students towards success.

In addition to academics, we encourage students to engage in extracurricular activities that promote personal growth and leadership skills. Our college community is built on the principles of inclusivity, respect, and excellence.
